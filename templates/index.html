How it works

From submission to feedback in minutes.

01

Upload Submissions

Educators upload student work alongside rubrics and assessment specifications.

02

AI Generates Feedback

The system analyses each submission against the rubric and produces detailed, question-level feedback and marks.

03

Review & Approve

Educators review, edit, and approve AI-generated feedback before it is released to students.

04

Students View Results

Students access their finalised feedback and download reports — all in one place.
